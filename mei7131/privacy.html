9. Controlling Do Not Track

Currently, there is no uniform technology standard for recognizing and implementing DNT signals, so we do not respond to DNT signals or other automatic mechanisms for opting out of online tracking. If we adopt future online tracking standards that we need to follow, we will inform you in a revised version of this privacy policy.
